Common questions

Is it really free?

Yes. Every lesson, exercise, worksheet and certificate. No trial, no locked content, no ads.

I have never written code. Can I do this?

You are exactly who Stage 1 was written for. It assumes nothing — not even knowing what a file is. Thousands of words of it exist purely to explain things other courses skip.

What do I need?

Any browser, on a phone or computer. Python runs inside the page. Later lessons show you how to set up real tools on a computer, when you are ready.

Will this get me a job?

Honest answer: the course takes you to genuinely employable skills — Python, SQL, data analysis and AI engineering are what real job listings ask for. But finishing lessons alone hires nobody; the course ends with four portfolio projects, and those plus interview preparation are what convert learning into offers.

Can I build something like ChatGPT after this?

You will understand how it works and be able to build real applications on top of models like it — which is what AI jobs actually are. Building a foundation model from scratch needs a research lab and tens of millions of dollars; any course claiming to teach that is selling something.
